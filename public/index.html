The Coffee Log

Send us an email; we’ll send updates from your favorite sites every morning.

  1. Email Us

    Send an email to subscribe@thecoffeelog.com with the URLs of the sites you want to follow. Don't worry, you can remove or change them at any time.

  2. Check Your Inbox

    In a few moments, you’ll receive and email from us with updates from your sites and a link to manage your subscriptions.

  3. Confirm Your Subscriptions

    Follow the link included in the email to activate your account and update your subscriptions.
